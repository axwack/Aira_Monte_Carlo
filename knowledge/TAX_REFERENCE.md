## Federal Standard Deduction (2026)
- Base (MFJ): **$32,200**
- Additional per spouse age 65+: **+$1,650**
- Both spouses 65+: total deduction = **$35,500**

## Social Security Taxation
- Up to **85%** of Social Security benefits are included in federal AGI, depending on provisional income.
- **New Jersey** fully exempts Social Security from state tax.

## Qualified Charitable Distribution (QCD) Limit (2026)
- **$111,000** per person per year (indexed from $105,000 in 2024).
- Applies from age 70½ onward; not relevant until Vin turns 70½ in 2040.
- Indexed annually at ~2.5%.
