# Roth Conversion Rules
- RMD age 75 (SECURE 2.0)
- Withdrawal order: taxable → pre-tax → Roth (only pre-tax counts as ordinary income)
- Bracket fill: room = target top – taxable before conversion; conv = min(room, pre-tax balance)
- Overrides: 2030: $0, 2034: $0, 2035: $0
- IRMAA guard: age 60-65 max 22%
- Tax funding: outside_cash → full conversion to Roth
